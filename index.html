How has air quality changed in the past decade in NYC?

There are a plethora of factors that contribute to air quality in New York City. One of them being particulate matter that can be dangerous in prolong exposure. Particulate matter 2.5, in specific, can cause issues in children and adults with prexisting health conditions. Some of the health risks assocatiated with high levels to PM2.5 include, but are not limited to:

  • Increased hospital admissions for heart or lung causes
  • Acute and chronic bronchitis
  • asthma attacks
  • emergency room visits
  • respiratory symptoms
  • restricted acitivity days

According to this gothamist news article, air quality is improving in New York City, although at a slow rate and at a rate in which the WHO still deems dangerous.
